About Our Center

The Libyan Elite University Training Center is dedicated to providing world-class professional education in Benghazi. We bridge the gap between academic knowledge and industry requirements.

Mission

To empower individuals and organizations with cutting-edge skills and knowledge that drive professional excellence and contribute to Libya's economic development.

Vision

To become the premier training institution in North Africa, recognized for innovation, quality, and industry partnerships.

Who Should Attend?

  • Students: Enhance your academic learning with practical skills
  • Professionals: Upskill and stay competitive in your field
  • Organizations: Custom corporate training solutions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our programs

You can enroll by visiting our contact section below, calling our office, or sending an email. Our enrollment team will guide you through the registration process and help you choose the right program for your needs.

Program costs vary by course duration and specialization. Faculty courses range from 800-1200 LYD, trending topics from 1500-2500 LYD, and summer training is 600 LYD. We offer flexible payment plans for qualified candidates.

Upon successful completion, you will receive a Libyan Elite University Training Center certificate accredited by the university. Specialized programs also include industry-recognized certifications where applicable.

Prerequisites vary by program. Most faculty courses have minimal prerequisites, while advanced trending topics may require specific background knowledge. Each program page lists detailed requirements.

Currently, most programs are offered in-person to ensure hands-on experience. However, hybrid options are available for select courses. Contact us to discuss available online learning options.
